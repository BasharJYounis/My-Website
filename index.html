Cyber Security Department at JUST Website

My first project was a website for the Cyber Security Department at Jordan University of Science and Technology, which includes three majors: Cyber Security, Internet of Things, and Game Design and Development. The website offers many features designed to support students, whether they are new or senior, by helping them on multiple levels, especially academically.

Some of the most important features of the website include a university GPA calculator, study resources for all three majors, dedicated communities for each major, and simplified methods to facilitate communication with the department’s professors.

I completed this project in 2025 while I was a second-year student majoring in Cyber Security at Jordan University of Science and Technology, under the supervision of Dr. Qasem Abu Al-Heijah.
